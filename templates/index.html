Did You Know?

Brain Tumor Types

There are over 120 different types of brain tumors. Our AI can detect 4 major types: Glioma, Meningioma, Pituitary tumors, and healthy brain tissue.

Early Detection

Early detection of brain tumors can improve treatment outcomes by up to 90%. MRI scans combined with AI analysis provide rapid and accurate diagnosis.

AI Accuracy

Our AI models achieve over 99% accuracy in brain tumor classification, matching and sometimes exceeding human radiologist performance.

Global Impact

Brain tumors affect over 700,000 people worldwide annually. AI-powered diagnosis can help reduce diagnostic time from weeks to minutes.

Treatment Success

When detected early, some brain tumors have survival rates exceeding 95%. AI assistance helps doctors make faster, more accurate treatment decisions.

Accessibility

AI-powered diagnosis can bring expert-level medical analysis to remote areas and developing countries where specialist radiologists may not be available.
